InfiBITS

InfiBITS is a project on modular robotics. The goal of InfiBITS is to make many modules which are completely automated to decide which shape they have to take in a particular situation. Such robots can be used for different purposes and different situations. Damage in one [art of the Robot does not effect the working of whole robot in such robots. It is a reasearch project and is being handeled by 17 like minded people including me.It is in the development stage as of now.
